## Executive summary

The uploaded report presents CP8 as a GPT-2-class geometric transformer experiment and CP9 as a proposed multimodal extension. The document frames the system as a structured-data transformer research program focused on geometric sequence modeling, symbolic-to-visual grounding, and neural-symbolic verification.

## CP8 summary

CP8 is described as a 124M-parameter GPT-2-class autoregressive transformer using:

- 12 transformer layers
- 12 attention heads
- 768-dimensional embeddings
- 512-token context window
- 1,024-symbol sacred-geometry glyph vocabulary
- next-glyph prediction objective

The report's main CP8 thesis is that structured geometric training data can induce attention-head specialization, recursive construction learning, and output-distribution constraints without explicit group-theoretic architecture.

## CP9 summary

CP9 is described as a 254M-parameter multimodal extension combining:

- frozen CP8 symbolic stream with trainable LoRA adapters
- Vision Transformer visual stream
- Graph Neural Network coordinate/edge stream
- CLIP-style contrastive alignment
- neural-symbolic geometric verifier
- staged training curriculum

The goal of CP9 is to connect symbolic geometric sequences with rendered diagrams and metric coordinate constraints.
